Dog “Steals” Runner's Phone in Cocal

February 12, 2026 • Cocal, Piauí

A playful dog named Simba surprised a runner during a training session in Cocal, in northern Piauí. The dog grabbed her cellphone and ran away while the camera was recording.

The athlete, Thais Carvalho, said Simba often follows her during morning exercises. At first, she thought the phone had fallen on the road or inside the grass.

After searching for the device, she found Simba playing nearby with the cellphone. The phone was dirty with mud and saliva, but still working perfectly.

Later, when checking the gallery, Thais discovered that the camera had recorded Simba’s funny escape. The video became popular online because of the dog’s playful behavior.
