Crater Blocks Part of BR-343 in Northern Piauí

April 17, 2026 • By Eric Souza, g1 PI

Heavy rain caused a large crater on BR-343 between Buriti dos Lopes and Parnaíba in northern Piauí. Part of the highway was blocked after erosion damaged the shoulder of the road.

According to the Federal Highway Police (PRF), drivers can use alternative routes through the cities of Cocal and Bom Princípio do Piauí.

Authorities explained that intense rain caused the collapse of a drainage structure under the highway. The area was isolated to prevent accidents and protect drivers.

Engineers from DNIT are working to repair the damage. Officials believe traffic may return to normal in the next few days after emergency repairs.
