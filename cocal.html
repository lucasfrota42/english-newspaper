Cocal Highlights Historic Railway Station

January 24, 2026 • g1 PI / TV Clube

The city of Cocal, in northern Piauí, is becoming a popular destination for visitors interested in history and culture. The city is also known as “Cocal da Estação” because of its famous railway station.

One of the main attractions is the historic train station, which is one of the oldest railway stations in the state of Piauí.

In 2018, the station was renovated and transformed into a cultural and leisure center for the community. Today, the place attracts tourists and residents who want to learn more about the city’s history.

Besides the historic station, Cocal is also known for its natural beauty, peaceful atmosphere and welcoming people.

Local authorities hope tourism will continue growing in the region during the next few years.
