Thief Forgets Dog After Robbery

February 9, 2026 • Cocal, Piauí

A man invaded a house in Cocal and stole a gas cylinder and a bicycle. However, he accidentally forgot his dog inside the house.

The victim discovered the crime after waking up and seeing an unknown dog inside the residence. Neighbors later identified the suspect.

According to police, the dog helped the victim find the suspect’s house. The man was arrested shortly after the incident.
