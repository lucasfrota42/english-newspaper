Dog “Steals” Runner's Phone in Cocal

A runner from Cocal had a funny surprise during her training session after a dog named Simba grabbed her cellphone and ran away with it.

The dog accidentally recorded the entire escape while carrying the phone in his mouth. The video quickly became popular online.

According to the athlete, Simba often follows her during warmups and likes attention. Fortunately, the phone survived the adventure.
