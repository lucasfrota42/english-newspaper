Dog “Steals” Runner’s Phone in Cocal

February 12, 2026 • By Vitória Bacelar, g1 PI

A runner from Cocal, Piauí, had a funny surprise during her morning training session. A playful dog named Simba grabbed her phone and ran away with it.

The athlete, Thais Carvalho, said Simba often follows her during warm-ups before running. This time, however, he decided to “steal” her phone and play with it.

Thais became worried because she thought the phone had fallen on the road or broken. Later, she found Simba lying near the house with the device under his paws.

When she checked the gallery, she discovered the phone was still recording. The video showed Simba’s entire adventure while running away with the phone.

According to Thais, the phone was covered in dirt, grass and saliva, but luckily it was still working perfectly.

Simba’s owner later confirmed that the dog is always playful and likes to cause trouble at home too.
