“Dark Horse” Could Break Brazilian Cinema Records

May 14, 2026 • Omelete

The movie “Dark Horse”, a biographical film about former Brazilian president Jair Bolsonaro, may become the most expensive movie ever produced in Brazil.

Reports say the production received around R$ 61 million from businessman Daniel Vorcaro. The original budget was reportedly even higher.

The movie’s budget is larger than several famous Brazilian films, including Oscar-nominated productions such as “Cidade de Deus” and “Ainda Estou Aqui”.

The project has attracted attention because of both its political theme and its unusually high production costs.
