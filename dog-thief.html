Thief Forgets Dog After Robbery

February 9, 2026 • Cocal, Piauí

A man invaded a house in Cocal and stole a gas cylinder and a bicycle. However, he accidentally forgot his dog inside the house after the robbery.

The victim discovered the crime after waking up and seeing an unknown dog inside the residence. Neighbors later informed him that a suspicious man had been seen carrying a gas cylinder nearby.

According to police, the dog helped the victim locate the suspect’s house. When confronted, the suspect attacked the victim before being arrested hours later.

The unusual story became popular in the region because of the dog’s unexpected role in helping identify the criminal.
