Thief “Forgets” Dog After Robbery in Cocal

February 2026 • By Eduarda Barradas, g1 PI

A strange robbery surprised residents in the city of Cocal, in northern Piauí. A man invaded a house and stole a gas cylinder and a bicycle, but accidentally left his dog inside the home.

According to police, the suspect entered the house with the animal and later left alone carrying the stolen items. The resident only discovered the robbery after waking up and finding an unknown dog inside the house.

Neighbors told the victim they had seen a man walking nearby carrying a gas cylinder. Police said the dog eventually guided the victim to the suspect’s home.

When the victim confronted the suspect and asked for the items back, the man allegedly attacked him with a piece of wood and a knife. The victim suffered a broken arm and injuries to his hand.

The suspect was arrested later that day and taken to the police station. Authorities said he was already known for small crimes in the area.

There is still no information about where the dog is now. Many people online joked that the animal became an unexpected witness to the crime.
