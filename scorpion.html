English Teacher Survives Scorpion Sting

April 2026 • Special Local Story

An English teacher from Piauí surprised students after being stung by a scorpion earlier this week. Fortunately, she recovered quickly and is doing well.

According to friends, the teacher remained calm during the situation and even joked about becoming “the Scorpion Teacher” after the accident.

Students later created funny drawings and memes inspired by superheroes and video game characters. One cartoon version showed the teacher with scorpion powers similar to Mortal Kombat fighters.

Despite the scare, she returned to class normally and continued teaching English with her usual energy. The story quickly became popular among students and on social media.

Health professionals reminded the public to stay careful during hot weather, when scorpions are more common in homes and urban areas.
