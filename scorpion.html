English Teacher Survives Scorpion Sting

A teacher from Cocal became well known among students after surviving a scorpion sting. The incident happened earlier this month and quickly became a funny topic at school.

After recovering, students started jokingly calling her the “Scorpion Teacher”. Some even compared her to the Mortal Kombat character Scorpion because of the yellow outfit and the story.

Fortunately, the teacher recovered very well and returned to work normally a few days later. Students said she handled the situation calmly and with good humor.

The story became popular among local students and inspired memes and fan art online.
