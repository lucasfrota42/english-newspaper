English Teacher Survives Scorpion Sting

April 2026 • Cocal, Piauí

An English teacher from Cocal became well known among students after being stung by a scorpion. Fortunately, she received medical attention quickly and recovered without serious complications.

After the incident, students started making jokes and calling her “Scorpion Teacher”. The story became popular at school and on social media.

To make the situation more fun, a cartoon version of the teacher inspired by the character Scorpion from Mortal Kombat was created for the school newspaper.

Despite the scare, the teacher returned to class normally and continued teaching English to her students.
